Inheritance & Sudden Wealth

What To Do When Money Arrives All At Once

Inheritance · Settlement · Business Sale · Insurance Payout

Inheriting money is not the same as earning it. Whether the event is the passing of a parent, a legal settlement, the sale of a business, or a life insurance payout, sudden wealth arrives with grief, complexity, and decisions that cannot be undone.

We help clients in Oklahoma City navigate the first 90 days with clarity — tax implications, estate coordination, holding strategies, and the emotional weight of managing what you did not ask to receive. Our goal is to help you make decisions you will still feel good about in ten years.

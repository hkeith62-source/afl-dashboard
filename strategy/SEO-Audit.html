Internal SEO & Performance Audit Report

Advance Financial Lighthouse, Inc.
Prepared by: Keith
Date: April 2026

Executive Summary

This audit evaluates the website, Google Business Profile, local visibility, performance, content structure, and competitive landscape. The site is visually strong but held back by three core issues:

  • Mobile performance delays causing blank‑screen stalls before content appears.
  • Weak local prominence signals, resulting in no map‑pack visibility.
  • Thin content depth limiting organic visibility and authority.

These issues stem from platform limitations and incomplete local optimization, not user error. “The site is visually strong but operationally underpowered.”

1. Technical Performance Analysis

1.1 Overview

Desktop performance is strong, but mobile performance is slow enough to impact user experience. “Mobile performance is slow enough to impact user experience and engagement.”

1.2 Mobile Performance Findings

Real‑world tests revealed:

  • Long delays before anything appears
  • Blank‑screen stalls
  • Slow first paint
  • Slow hero image load
PageSpeed Insights (Mobile)
FCP: 6.7s
LCP: 18.2s
Speed Index: 9.5s
Performance Score: 56

1.3 Desktop Performance

GTmetrix (Desktop)
Performance: 99%
LCP: 811ms
TBT: 12ms

1.4 Root Cause

The bottleneck is FMG’s platform architecture: large JavaScript bundles, render‑blocking scripts, animation libraries, and slow initial server response. “These are platform‑level limitations, not user‑level errors.”

1.5 Business Impact

Slow mobile load times reduce trust, engagement, conversions, and search visibility.

1.6 Recommendations

  • Switch to a lighter FMG template
  • Compress hero images
  • Remove unnecessary animations
  • Defer or async non‑critical scripts
2. Local SEO Analysis

2.1 Overview

The Google Business Profile exists but lacks the completeness and prominence needed to rank locally.

2.2 GBP Findings

Missing elements include photos, posts, Q&A, services list, and business description. “This places the profile in the lowest completeness tier.”

2.3 Map Pack Visibility

The business does not appear for major category searches such as “financial advisor Oklahoma City.”

2.4 Address Search Test

Searching the full address returns no recognized place entity. “Google has not fully associated the business with its physical location.”

2.5 Review Profile

4 reviews (5.0 rating). Competitors often have 20–100+ reviews.

2.6 Recommendations

  • Add 10–20 high‑quality photos
  • Increase review volume
  • Add services list and business description
  • Publish monthly GBP posts
  • Build local citations
3. Content & On‑Site Structure

3.1 Overview

The site is visually polished but content‑thin. “Service pages are short… no supporting content… no internal linking strategy.”

3.2 Recommendations

  • Expand service pages
  • Add FAQs and internal links
  • Add educational content
  • Add case studies or examples
4. Competitive Landscape

Competitors have stronger GBP profiles, more reviews, more photos, deeper content, and stronger category signals.

5. Summary of Recommendations

  • Compress hero images
  • Defer scripts

Local SEO

  • Increase reviews
  • Add services list & description
  • Build citations

Content

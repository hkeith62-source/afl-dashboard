1. What This Plan Is About

This strategy is the one master plan for improving Advance Financial Lighthouse's visibility on Google. It covers everything: titles, descriptions, keywords, content, technical fixes, backlinks, social media, reviews, and a 9-month schedule.

"This is the single, authoritative SEO strategy and action plan… No other document is needed."

2. What We Found

Kathy already has 30+ years of credibility, but none of it is connected to the website.

  • LinkedIn articles don't link to the website.
  • Media mentions don't link back.
  • No blog.
  • No online scheduling.
  • No Facebook Business Page.
  • Page titles use inconsistent brand names ("Financial Lighthouse" instead of "Advance Financial Lighthouse").
  • Several pages use ALL CAPS titles.
  • Meta descriptions are outdated or missing entirely.
  • NAP (Name, Address, Phone) is not consistent in the site footer.
  • Only 4 Google reviews (5 stars — strong foundation, but low volume).
  • BBB profile exists but is not accredited or rated.
  • Instagram account exists but is disconnected from the website.

"These assets are currently disconnected from your website… The opportunity is clear: connecting the authority you have already built to the website you already own."

3. What We Recommend (10 Priorities)

  1. Fix page titles and descriptions.
  2. Add online scheduling.
  3. Run a technical audit of FMG Suite.
  4. Gather all authority assets and reclaim backlinks.
  5. Create a Facebook Business Page.
  6. Move LinkedIn strategy so articles start on the website.
  7. Build an Oklahoma-focused keyword strategy.
  8. Publish 1–2 strong articles per month.
  9. Grow Google Reviews.
  10. Track everything with Search Console and Analytics.

"This plan addresses ten areas, in priority order…"

4. Timeline

Weeks 1–2: Fix titles, add scheduling, set up analytics, create Facebook Page, add NAP to footer.
Weeks 3–4: Technical audit + authority inventory.
Month 2: First article + keyword validation.
Months 3–6: Publish content, grow reviews, distribute on social.
Months 7–9: Optimize based on data.

Results timeline:
- On-page fixes (titles, descriptions) start affecting search results within 2–4 weeks.
- Content and backlinks take 3–6 months to produce significant ranking movement.
- Consistent execution over 6–12 months is required for sustained improvement.
